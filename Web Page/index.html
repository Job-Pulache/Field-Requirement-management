01 — Problem

What was broken?

In agro-export operations, requirement requests moved verbally or on paper — no traceability, no SLA, no visibility for management.

node_01

No digital record

Requests were made verbally or on handwritten forms with no way to track status or history.

node_02

No approval SLA

No defined maximum time for management to respond — causing delays that impacted field operations.

node_03

No rejection tracking

When a request was rejected the reason was never recorded — the same issues repeated across every campaign.

node_04

Logistics had no visibility

Logistics only learned of approved requests via physical notification — creating last-minute bottlenecks.

05 — Improvements

From AS-IS to TO-BE

Four concrete improvements identified through process analysis and implemented in the redesigned flow.

01

Digital Request Registration

Every request is now logged digitally from the start, enabling full traceability and historical analysis.

02

24-Hour Response SLA

Management now has a defined maximum response window, reducing operational delays in the field.

03

Rejection Reason Tracking

When rejected, the reason is recorded and communicated back to the field — enabling continuous improvement.

04

Automatic Logistics Notification

Upon approval, logistics receives an automatic notification — eliminating last-minute bottlenecks.
